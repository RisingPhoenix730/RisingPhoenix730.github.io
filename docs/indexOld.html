About

I am a developer based in Burtchville, MI, USA. I specialize in Full Stack development and software design. I love working with code, writing clean, clear, and concise code that functions correctly makes my day a little brighter. I have a passion for creating solutions and helping people. I believe it is important and almost essential for companies to have a presence on the internet in today's world, and while AI can do a lot, writing clean and reusable code that can be easily understood by a human is not yet one of those things. I love a challenge, and getting a program to work after a struggle with can be extremely rewarding.
